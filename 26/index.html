PICT B.E. Course Structure

First Year (FE)
Mathematics I
Introduction to calculus, algebra, and trigonometry.
Physics
Fundamentals of physics including mechanics and waves.
Second Year (SE)
Data Structures
Introduction to algorithms and data structures.
Digital Electronics
Study of digital circuits and logic gates.
Third Year (TE)
Operating Systems
Study of OS concepts, processes, and file management.
Database Management Systems
Fundamentals of databases, SQL, and database design.
Final Year (BE)
Software Engineering
Study of software development processes and methodologies.
Machine Learning
Introduction to machine learning algorithms and techniques.
